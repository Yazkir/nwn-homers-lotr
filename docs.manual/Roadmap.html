Roadmap & Player Ideas — Homer's LOTR

As of June 22nd, 2026 This roadmap is a living document and changes often — costs, priorities and plans are all subject to change.

This is the living development roadmap for Homer's LOTR. Almost everything here started as a player suggestion or bug report — when an idea ships, the player who submitted it earns Merit points to spend on the rewards below. Have an idea or found a bug? Tell a DM in game or on Discord; if it makes the cut, you get the credit.

Almost every entry below is tagged with the player who suggested it. Shipped items earned that player Merit points; in-progress items are what's being worked on or queued next. New here? See the Customizations page for how the rules differ from stock NWN.

Earning & Spending Merit

Report a bug that gets fixed, or suggest a feature that ships, and you earn Merit. Spend it on the rewards below.

Player Housing

Turn an unused town door into your own home.

Claim any unoccupied locked door in any town as your home (1 per player / CD key; moving or upgrading later refunds nothing). You build the area in the toolset and send the file; a fast-travel option to it is added to your rest menu. You teleport in, and leave through the front door to your chosen city.
