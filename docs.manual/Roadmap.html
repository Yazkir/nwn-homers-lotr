Roadmap & Player Ideas — Homer's LOTR

As of June 23rd, 2026 at 07:38 CDT

This is the living development roadmap for Homer's LOTR. Almost everything here started as a player suggestion or bug report — when an idea ships, the player who submitted it earns Merit points to spend on the rewards documented here. Have an idea or found a bug? Tell a DM in game or on Discord; if it makes the cut, you get the credit.

Roadmap — In Progress & Up Next

Where the module is heading, grouped by feature. "In progress" is being actively worked; "Up next" is queued, with "Soon" and "Later" progressively further out; "Under consideration" is captured but not yet committed to; "Not likely to implement" is logged but probably won't happen.
