  Here's the full faction bug analysis, confirmed against source blueprints and

  ---
  Faction Bug Report

  How the alignment system works (for context)

  Faction 5 (Evil) is the faction players join when they choose the Evil orb.
  Evil-faction NPCs have rep 100 toward Evil players (friendly). Hostile-faction
   NPCs have rep 0 toward all factions including Evil вҖ” they attack everyone
  regardless of alignment.

  ---
  Bug 1: Dol Guldur City (South of Mirkwood) вң… confirmed

  dolguldurcity.git.json has 21 placed creatures:
  - akhorahil004 (Orc General) вҖ” faction 5 (Evil) вң“ correct
  - akhorahil006 (Morgrond Captain) вҖ” faction 5 (Evil) вң“ correct
  - mordororcbowm005 Г— 10 (Dol-Guldur Elite Bow Man) вҖ” faction 1 (Hostile) вқҢ
  - creature041 Г— 9 (Dol-Guldur Defender) вҖ” faction 1 (Hostile) вқҢ

  The 19 guards are in Hostile faction (always attacks everyone), while the two
  command NPCs are correctly Evil. This also blocks access to the Dol Guldur
  Dark Smith shop via the Dol2Smith trigger in that area вҖ” which explains the
  "evil shopkeeper" report. The shop itself (dolguldursmi) is correctly in
  Merchant faction, but you can't reach it without fighting through 19 Hostile
  guards.

  Fix: Change mordororcbowm005.utc.json and creature041.utc.json FactionID from
  1 to 5. These blueprints only appear in Dol Guldur City so there are no other
  placements to worry about.

  ---
  Bug 2: Numenor areas вң… confirmed

  All three Numenor areas (heavensdisdain, heavensscorn, falseheaven) have zero 
  placed creatures вҖ” they're all encounter pools. The pool blueprints:
  - nathanu014 (NГәmenorian Barbaric Warlord) вҖ” faction 1 (Hostile) вқҢ
  - storm (North NГәmenorian Arch Angel) вҖ” faction 1 (Hostile) вқҢ
  - summanus (Summanus) вҖ” faction 1 (Hostile) вқҢ

  These appear only in Numenor encounter pools. They should be Evil faction so
  evil-aligned players can navigate Numenor.

  Fix: Change FactionID from 1 вҶ’ 5 in nathanu014.utc.json, storm.utc.json, and
  summanus.utc.json.

  ---
  Bug 3: Edoras / Theoden's Chosen gate вҖ” design issue, not a simple faction fix

  The NPCs themselves are correctly factioned. The flow is:
  - Edoras вҶ’ (edoras2helm trigger) вҶ’ Path to Helm's Deep вҶ’ Helm's Deep: Cave
  (Theoden's Chosen)
  - All 13 Rohirrim in Path to Helm's Deep: faction 5 (Evil) вң“
  - All 12 creatures in Helm's Deep Cave: faction 5 (Evil) вң“

  The problems are:
  1. The edoras2helm trigger is inside Edoras, which has 16 Good-faction guards.
   Evil-aligned players are attacked on sight by Good NPCs (Evil <-> Good = 0),
  so they can't peacefully reach the transition.
  2. Locked door: Wall2Gate (locked=1, no key tag) in Path to Helm's Deep. This
  is presumably lockpickable/bashable, but there's no clear opening mechanism
  for evil-aligned players.

  The player's experience of "always hostile" near Edoras is actually the Edoras
   Good-faction guards correctly attacking their Evil-aligned character вҖ” but
  the design traps evil players out of their own reward area. The fix would be
  either moving the edoras2helm transition to outside the Edoras guard
  perimeter, or adding an alternate entrance that doesn't require walking
  through the guarded town.

  ---
  Additional Dol Guldur inconsistencies (bonus)
