What makes this server unique

Powerlevel to 60

A custom HGLL leveling framework takes you all the way to level 60, with 1.5× XP so you spend more time building and fighting and less time grinding.

Build experimentation

Multiclass up to 4 classes per character and respec your look freely. There's no single "right" build — the fun is in trying combinations no one else has.

Item builds matter

Gear is as important as class here. The legendary loot scattered across Middle-earth can make or break a build — plan your equipment as carefully as your levels.

Defeat the legends

Hunt iconic characters from the LotR universe as boss fights across the Shire, Bree, Moria, Lothlórien, Rohan, Gondor, Mordor and beyond.

Open PvP

Feeling bold? PvP is open almost anywhere. Test your build against other players, not just the monsters — if you're up for it.

Play your way

Free appearance, weapon-FX and alignment changing, built on CEP 2 with expanded creatures, placeables and gear. Make a character that's truly yours.

A server shaped by its players

This module is actively developed and built around player ideas. Almost every feature and fix on the server started as a suggestion or bug report from someone like you — and when your idea ships, you earn Merit points to spend on teleports, premium boosts, player housing and more.

The Roadmap & Player Ideas page is the living record of that: everything recently shipped (with credit to whoever suggested it) and a grouped roadmap of what's in progress and coming next.

Got an idea or found a bug? Catch a DM in-game if we're online at the same time — but the most reliable way to reach me is the #feature-requests and #bugs channels on Discord, where nothing gets lost.
