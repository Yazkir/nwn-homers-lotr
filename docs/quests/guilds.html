1final=== Guilds === Tired of having parties...wanna make something big with many people? Then you can make a Guild. Keep it safe and no swearing. If you wanna create you own Guild then you must undergo these tasks; 1* Be in the Hall of Legends 2* You must have the ability to run and maintain your Guild or else it WILL BE disbanded. 3* Your Guild must have an ACCEPTABLE name, speak to a DM about it. 4* Find a CO Leader to join you in your quest to create a Guild...CO Leaders must undergo tasks 1 and 5 . 5* All your characters will be deleted but your MAIN Guild Leader character. That will be the MAIN MAIN MAIN character you will use for any Guild affiliation. Before becoming a leader, you may transfer your gear to your MAIN before getting your other characters deleted. Your MAIN MUST LOOK GOOD, and once your MAIN is created, you will automatically get 40 lvls for your MAIN only. After all this, you may create new characters but they must have your Guild name on.(((THIS WILL HAPPEND AFTER MAKE YOUR GUILD))) If you dont understand this then E-Mail Lord Deems with your question. Features of Creating a Guild; Once you create your Guild, then you may purchase a Lair for your Guild, there members may meet and socialize and do whatever. Also, with a Lair you get access to special things. To see a DEMO of a normal Guild Lair please visit the Lair Area. Guild Leaders will receive a 'special item' , only 4 will be in the server. Also Leaders will get DM Powers(Lesser) to help maintaining the Guild. Leaders may make their owns Ranking system and give power to members. There is so MUCH things you can do as a Leader of a Guild.....its great! Joining a Guild.... Joining a Guild will be determined by the Leader and CO Leader of the Guild. DMs cannot interfere with any Guild activity or affiliation, unless permitted by a Lord Deems or a Leader of the Guild. Wanna increase your chances of joining a Guild? Then try hitting to be in Hall of Legends!hgll_cliententer
