Use shout frequently and for GOOD purposes. 3) If you loose items, please talk to a DM about it. Remember you won't always get the items you lost back. So unless your reason is REASONABLE then you will be helped. No not complaining! 4) And when in a Guild PLEASE YOU MUST FOLLOW SERVER RULES 1- 10 + Guild RULES!!!!
