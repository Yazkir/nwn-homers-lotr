1final=== Good/Evil Factions === Two factions: Good (Gondor, Rivendell, Lothlorien) and Evil (Mordor, Isengard). D&D alignment on your character sheet has NO effect on NPC hostility — it is cosmetic only. Set your faction each session in the Well of Eru by clicking an orb: Shaft of Light (white) = Good Shaft of Light (red) = Evil Use Guardian of Light or Guardian of Darkness to reach faction-appropriate teleport hubs.
