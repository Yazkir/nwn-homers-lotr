entry #5 ¶
Making bets is simple, just tell me what you'd like to bet on, and I'll put it on the table for you. The format for telling me bets is to say "amount | bet type | numbers" (using the conversation bar, where it says 'Say:'). Valid betting amounts are 1, 5, 10, 50, 100, 500, 1000, 5000, and 10000 gold. Any bet is possible, as multiple amounts on the same bet stack. Valid betting types are Number, Split, Street, Corner, Five, Line, Dozen, Column, Odd, Even, Red, Black, High and Low. For example, a 100 gold bet on the number 2 would be "100 Number 2" and 50 gold bet on Odds would be "50 Odd".

entry #6 ¶
The 'numbers' field isn't necessary for Odd, Even, Red, Black, High, Low, and Five bets. For a Number bet, just put the number you'd like to bet on after 'Number'. Valid numbers include 1 through 36, 0 and 00. For a Split bet, just put the two numbers that you'd like to bet on with the lowest number first. (They have to be adjacent to eachother on the table.) For a Column or Dozen bet, valid numbers are 1, 2 and 3. For Columns, the numbers correspond to the top number in the column. For Dozens, the numbers correspond to 1-12, 13-24, and 25-36.

entry #7 ¶
For a corner bet, you'll need to put the highest and lowest number in the corner with a dash inbetween, such as 1-5 or 2-6. Note that this bet doesn't cover all numbers in the range, just the ones that make a square. (1-5 doesn't include 3, and 2-6 doesn't include 4, etc.). The same applies to Line and Street bets, though all the numbers in the range are included.

entry #2 ¶
There are several types of bets, with different odds of hitting. The first type of bet is a Red/Black, Odd/Even, or Low/High bet. The payoff of these bets is 1:1, and they cover half of the numbers on the table, not including 0 and 00. The second type of bet is a Column bet or a Dozen bet. The payoff of these bets is 2:1, and they cover 12 numbers on the table, either one of the groups of 12 numbers numerically, or the 12 numbers included on one column of the table..

entry #3 ¶
The third type of bet is called a Line bet, and it covers 6 numbers included in two adjacent rows of the table. Its payoff is 5:1. The fourth type of bet is the Five-Number bet, and it covers the numbers 0, 00, 1, 2, and 3. Its payoff is 6:1. The fifth type of bet is a Corner bet, which covers four numbers that make a square on the table. Its payoff is 8:1. The sixth type of bet is a Street bet, covering a single row of three numbers on the table and paying off at 11:1.
