Harp of the Angel's Heart is an instrument that only the best minstrels can use to its full potential. It is balanced in such a way that it can it can heal an hurt with equal effectiveness. It is said that many years ago a young girl was tending sheep when she heard a a loud peal of thunder even though the sky was clear. Suddenly a strange object hit the ground at such an enourmous rate it left a small crater. Peering cautiously inside, the young shepherd was dazzled by a golden light. When the heavenly light subsided, at the bottom of the crater lay a golden harp. The local priest advised that it be kept in his temple, from which it was stolen within a week. Since then it has been rumoured to be in the hands of a private collector in sarlo, waiting to be reawoken in the hands of a master harpist
