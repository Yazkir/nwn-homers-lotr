[BUG] Dol Guldur ramp door transition loop (dolguldurcity)
-----------------------------------------------------------
Reported by player: "The door transition in Dol Guldur is facing the wrong way,
so you end up on the ramp in the middle, where you can't progress forward."

Confirmed. Both doors `Dol62Dol7` and `Dol72Dol6` exist in the same area
(dolguldurcity.git.json, "Dol Guldur City"). They sit ~10 units apart on a ramp
(Y:45.08 and Y:54.92, Z:-0.34) and link to each other, creating a loop:

  Dol62Dol7  (bearing π/2, east)   LinkedTo: Dol72Dol6  → same area
  Dol72Dol6  (bearing -π/2, west)  LinkedTo: Dol62Dol7  → same area

Going through either door just bounces you to the other door 10 units away.
No forward progress is possible.

Root cause: the names suggest a two-area transition — Dol62Dol7 = "Dol 6 → Dol 7",
Dol72Dol6 = "Dol 7 → Dol 6". Dol62Dol7 belongs in dolguldurcity ("Dol 6") and
Dol72Dol6 should be in a separate "Dol 7" area that either no longer exists or
was never created.

Fix options:
  A) If "Dol 7" was a separate area that was removed: delete Dol72Dol6 from
     dolguldurcity.git.json and either remove Dol62Dol7 or re-link it to the
     correct destination area.
  B) If the ramp is meant to be a plain walkable passage (no area transition):
     delete both doors and let the player walk through freely.

[BUG] Item Forge never charges gold — constant mismatch in payment scripts
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Reported by player: "Item forge doesn't actually take the money for adding
magic to items."

Confirmed. Two-script mismatch:

calcmodvalue2.nss sets:
  iDiff = 8   when player must PAY  (comment says "iValue is amount to pay")
  iDiff = -8  when player gets REFUND

modifyitem.nss checks:
  if (iDiff == 1) TakeGoldFromCreature(...)   ← never true (8 ≠ 1)
  else if (iDiff == -1) GiveGoldToCreature(...)  ← never true (-8 ≠ -1)

Neither branch ever fires. Modifications are applied for free and refunds are
never paid out.

Fix: Change modifyitem.nss lines 10 and 12 to check iDiff == 8 and iDiff == -8
respectively. Or change calcmodvalue2.nss to set iDiff = 1 / -1.
The simpler and less risky change is modifyitem.nss (one file, two int literals):
  if (iDiff == 8) TakeGoldFromCreature(iValue, oPC, TRUE);
  else if (iDiff == -8) GiveGoldToCreature(oPC, iValue);
