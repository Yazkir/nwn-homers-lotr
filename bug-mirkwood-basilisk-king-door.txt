Player report: "a door in Mirkwood which should lead to Basilisk King, according to the wiki,
but it takes you to an empty tomb"

Investigation found TWO bugs.

========================================
BUG 1 — Orphaned broken door in Crypt Lower
========================================
File: unpacked/area002.git.json

Door with Tag=lower2crypt has LinkedTo=crypt2lower.
No door with tag crypt2lower exists anywhere in the module.
Wiki already labels it "(unresolved waypoint crypt2lower)".
In NWN1 this silently fails to transport or produces undefined engine behaviour.

Fix: Remove the lower2crypt door entry (Door List index 0) from area002.git.json.
The remaining two doors (lower2cata → area003, JeweledDoor → mirkwoodeast) are correct.


========================================
BUG 2 — Basilisk King encounter never fires (door is inside trigger polygon)
========================================
File: unpacked/area003.git.json

The cata2lower door (entry into Crypt Chamber from Crypt Lower) is at position (45.0, 80.0, 0.0).

The Basilisk King encounter trigger polygon covers approx x=26-64, y=26-85 (absolute).
The door at y=80 is INSIDE the trigger.

NWN1 encounter triggers fire OnEntered when a creature crosses the boundary from outside.
A player who materialises at a door inside the trigger never crosses inward → Basilisk King
never spawns → "empty tomb".

Fix: Shrink the trigger's top boundary to ~y=74 so the door at y=80 is outside it.

The encounter position is XPosition=27.349, YPosition=84.9153.
Geometry points are stored as RELATIVE offsets to that position.
Two top-edge vertices need updating in the Geometry list:

  Current G4: X=0.0,    Y=0.0     (absolute y ≈ 84.92)  → change Y to -10.9153  (absolute y ≈ 74)
  Current G5: X=0.4012, Y=-0.8023 (absolute y ≈ 84.11)  → change Y to -11.7176  (absolute y ≈ 73.2)

This pulls the top edge from y≈85 down to y≈74.
Player enters through door at y=80, walks toward room centre, crosses trigger at y≈74,
Basilisk King spawns at its existing spawn point (44.59, 41.72).
Bottom/side edges of trigger are unchanged.

Encounter config looks fine: Active=1, Reset=1, ResetTime=3000s, Respawns=-1.
